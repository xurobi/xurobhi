About us

Surabhi Agro Foods, Tezpur,Assam is a rural farmer's group engaged in traditional village agri-practices to provide Village Fresh Products directly to the consumers from their gardens.

Objective

Its main aim is to produce vegetables,fruits and other food products in the traditional village way vis-a-vis the organic way.

What we do?

Our cultivation is based on animal manures, organic wastes, crop rotations,legumes and aspects of biological pest control. We avoid the use of synthetically produced fertilizers, pesticides, growth regulators and livestock additives. We care for our soil rather than the crops to maintain optimum soil health with its vibrancy and resilience thus making the soil capable of supplying all the essential nutrients to the crop for its proper growth and development. We the farmers are aiming at sustaining and increasing the productivity by improving the soil health and over all improvement of agro-ecosystem. The food items are all fresh in nature with 100 percent guarantee in terms of purity as we care for the health of the people. We have now started delivery at home of our food items with the nominal delivery rate after finalizing your order by phone or email. When you order we pluck the items from the gardens and fields to deliver it to you fresh at your home.
